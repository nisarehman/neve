About Neve.

The project touches on the topic of menstruation and menstrual cramps. The concept of the product was born in it's natural way, because we are women that face these problems and want to improve our daily life. Neve is a smart wearable heating pad that uses a natural way of dealing pain. The product gives comfort and sense of being at home wherever you go. Its appearence reflects the active and meaningful lifestyle of women, emphasizes their power and style in coexistence with flexibility of the way of living.

  • The menstrual cramps can cause problems in working activities and concentration, so Neve is designed to keep the pain under control and go on with your daily activities.
  • During menstruation women feel so vulnerable and prefer to stay at home to have a rest.Neve conveys that feeling by warming painful parts of the body
  • The shape and the size of the product are determined by the pain spots and body ergonomics.

Neve can be used basically anywhere! It uses a natural way of pain relief - heating. Warming up body parts that ache and th etemperature used here is up to 40 °C is as effective as using ibuprofen. But healthier as well. ⠀

Frequenty Asked Questions

  • What is a Cramp Comforter?

    Cramp Comfort is a self-heating patch inspired by the function of a hot water bag for cramps during periods. The latest Neve product will not only offer relief to your period pain but also provide a portable solution to carry it outdoors.

  • How to use a Cramp Comforter?

    1.Open - Take the patch out of its packet.
    2.Stick - Place the patch in your pant liner.
    3.Relax - The patch will start heating up on its own. Leave it on for up to 8 hours, and then dispose.

  • How does Neve Cramp Comforter works?

    Inspired by a hot water bag, Neve is designed to fit your life! The patches emit soothing heat for up to 8 hours and hug your body to give you relief from cramps.
